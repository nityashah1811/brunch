Welcome to Moriahlinks

Moriahlinks is an international company incorporated in the US and in Nigeria to expose and connect the Nigerian farming industry to the international market and increase the value that local farmers get from selling their agricultural products to the international market.

We started Morialinks because we believe an equitable share of value to the farmers can bring about economic diversification, development, and advancement in Nigeria and Sub-Saharan Africa in general.

We intend to make export easier and available to local farmers of all sizes. Farmers can easily access the international market and get more value for their products. Thus, farmers can get more income and increase production which in turn will expand the farming industry in these localities
